Who We Are

A Community Built on Faith

Miracle Church Revival Fellowship (MRFC) is a Spirit-filled congregation committed to experiencing God's presence, lifting up the name of Jesus Christ, and empowering believers to live out their faith in everyday life.

We believe in the power of prayer, the authority of God's Word, and the transforming work of the Holy Spirit. Our doors are open to everyone — whether you're seeking God for the first time or growing deeper in your walk with Him.
