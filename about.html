Our Story

How MRFC Began

Miracle Church Revival Fellowship began with a vision from God — a burning desire to see souls saved, lives transformed, and communities renewed through the power of the Holy Spirit.

From our humble beginnings with a small group of faithful believers, MRFC has grown into a vibrant congregation that believes in miracles, revival, and the power of community. Our name reflects our conviction: God still performs miracles today, and revival is always possible through fervent prayer and faith.

We are a Pentecostal/Charismatic fellowship that embraces the gifts of the Holy Spirit, expository preaching, heartfelt worship, and community outreach. Every week, we gather with expectation — knowing that God is present and ready to move among His people.

Doctrine

What We Believe

1
The Holy Trinity

We believe in one God — Father, Son, and Holy Spirit — three persons in perfect unity.

2
The Bible

The Bible is the inspired, infallible Word of God, the ultimate authority for faith and life.

3
Salvation by Grace

Salvation is by grace alone, through faith alone, in Jesus Christ alone.

4
The Holy Spirit

We believe in the baptism of the Holy Spirit and the operation of spiritual gifts today.

5
The Church

The Church is the body of Christ, called to worship, fellowship, service, and witness.

6
Prayer & Worship

We believe in the power of prayer and corporate worship in Spirit and in truth.

7
The Second Coming

We believe in the literal, physical return of Jesus Christ in glory.

8
Divine Healing

We believe God heals the sick today — spirit, soul, and body.
