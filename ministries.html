Get Involved

There's a place for everyone in the body of Christ. Find your calling and serve with us!

Worship Ministry

Worship Team Leader

Every Sunday

Leading the congregation in heartfelt, Spirit-filled worship every service. We believe worship transforms hearts and ushers in God's presence.

Youth Ministry

Youth Pastor

Every Saturday, 3:00 PM

Empowering young people to pursue God, build their faith, and make a difference in the world. A generation on fire for Jesus!

Prayer Warriors

Prayer Ministry Head

Every Friday, 7:30 PM

A dedicated team of intercessors standing in the gap for our church, nation, and the world. We believe in the power of persistent prayer.

Women of God

Women's Ministry Leader

Every Thursday, 5:00 PM

Equipping women to walk in their God-given identity and purpose. A sisterhood rooted in faith, love, and the power of the Holy Spirit.

Men's Fellowship

Men's Ministry Head

Every Saturday, 7:00 AM

Building brotherhood, accountability, and godly leadership among the men of MRFC. Iron sharpens iron.

Children's Ministry

Children's Ministry Head

Every Sunday during service

Nurturing the faith of the youngest members of our church family. Teaching children to love Jesus through fun, creative, and impactful programs.

Outreach & Missions

Missions Coordinator

Monthly (varies)

Taking the Gospel beyond our walls — feeding programs, relief operations, evangelism, and supporting missionaries in the field.

Discipleship & Cell Groups

Discipleship Pastor

Weekly (various schedules)

Small group gatherings for deeper Bible study, fellowship, and spiritual growth. Connect with a cell group near you!
