Generosity

Support the Work of God

Your giving enables us to spread the Gospel, run our ministries, support missionaries, and serve our community. Every gift — big or small — makes a difference.

"Each of you should give what you have decided in your heart to give, not reluctantly or under compulsion, for God loves a cheerful giver."

— 2 Corinthians 9:7

Give In Person

Drop your offering or tithe during any of our Sunday or weekday services. Offering baskets are available during service.

GCash / Mobile

Send your offering via GCash. Contact the church office for our official GCash number and reference instructions.

Bank Transfer

You may also give via bank transfer. Please contact our church office for official bank account details.

What Your Giving Supports

Worship & Services

Funding weekly worship services and special events throughout the year.

Youth & Children Programs

Supporting programs that nurture the next generation of believers.

Community Outreach

Feeding programs, relief operations, and community service projects.

Missions & Evangelism

Spreading the Gospel locally and supporting missionaries abroad.

Church Facilities

Maintaining and improving our church building and equipment.

Pastoral Staff Support

Enabling our pastors and staff to serve the congregation full-time.
